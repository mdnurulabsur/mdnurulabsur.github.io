## Summary

PhD Candidate in Computer Science at CUNY Graduate Center. I work on edge intelligence, multimodal sensing, and resilient XR systems, with a focus on reliability, low-latency inference, and real-world deployment constraints.

---

## Research Interests

- Edge intelligence for vision and XR systems  
- Reliable multi-view 3D reconstruction in just-in-time edge environments  
- Multimodal sensing for privacy-preserving perception (vision + WiFi/CSI)  
- Security and robustness of offloaded perception pipelines  
- Resource-aware system design (latency, energy, bandwidth, QoE)
