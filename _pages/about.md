I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the CUNY Graduate Center, advised by Prof. Saptarshi Debroy. My work sits at the intersection of computer vision, edge systems, and networking. I design edge-centric pipelines for depth and multimodal sensing, 3D reconstruction, and XR, with a focus on **latency, robustness, and privacy**.

I like problems where the model is not enough: you have to care about hardware limits, bandwidth, and what happens under failures or attacks.

### Research areas

- Edge intelligence for vision and XR  
- Multimodal sensing (depth, CSI, inertial, audio)  
- Reliable, disruption-aware 3D reconstruction  
- Privacy-preserving human activity recognition  
- Disaster, healthcare, and safety-critical applications
