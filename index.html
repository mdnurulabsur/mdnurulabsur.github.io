About Me

I am a PhD candidate at CUNY focusing on real-world, privacy-preserving AI systems for edge devices. My research involves building multimodal pipelines using depth cameras and CSI signals to solve problems in human activity recognition, healthcare monitoring, and emergency response systems. I work at the intersection of edge computing, sensor fusion, and system design.

Projects

CSI+Depth Fusion for Edge HAR

Designed and implemented a privacy-first human activity recognition pipeline by fusing depth video with wireless CSI signals. Includes real-time deployment on Jetson TX2.

GitHub Repo

YOLOv8 + GY-521 Dual Sensor Road Stability System

Dual-stream vehicle stability framework combining computer vision and IMU sensor for on-road anomaly detection and control adjustment.

Tropical Cyclone Risk Modeling with ENSO & SST

Predictive modeling of Pacific cyclone events using multivariate anomaly data. Integrated trend analysis and real-world risk forecasting.
